Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

Advertisers can cancel their scheduled ad campaigns through the Ad Management dashboard. If a campaign is cancelled before it starts, the remaining budget remains in the account as ad credits.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are processed under the following circumstances:

  • An advertisement was rejected by our moderation team.
  • Technical failure on our platform prevented the ad from being served.
  • Double payment due to technical issues on the payment gateway.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Any budget already consumed for impressions, clicks, or video views is strictly non-refundable. Service fees, once consumed, are also non-refundable.

4. Refund Timeline and Process

To request a refund, please email factshort1@gmail.com with your Transaction ID. Once approved, refunds are initiated within 5-7 working days and credited back to the original payment method (Card, UPI, or Bank Account).

5. Duplicate Payments

In case of duplicate payments, the extra amount will be automatically refunded within 72 hours without requiring a manual request.
