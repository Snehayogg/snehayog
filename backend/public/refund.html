Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

Advertisers can cancel their ad campaigns at any time through the Ad Management dashboard. Once cancelled, the ad will stop serving within 24 hours.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only processed if the ad campaign was rejected by our moderation team or if there was a technical failure in serving the ad. Spent budget is non-refundable.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Any budget already consumed for delivered impressions or clicks is strictly non-refundable.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact factshort1@gmail.com/9518545858 with your campaign ID and reason for the refund. Valid refunds will be processed within 5-7 working days to the original payment method.

5. Duplicate Payments

In case of double payment due to technical issues, the extra amount will be refunded automatically within 72 hours.
