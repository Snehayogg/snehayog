Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

At Vayug, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we manage data for our content hosting and creator rewards platform.

1. Information Collection

We collect information necessary to provide a secure environment for creators:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details.
  • Content: Videos and media uploaded for hosting and distribution.
  • Payment & Billing: For users purchasing advertising credits, payments are processed via authorized third-party gateways. We do not store sensitive card data.
  • Payout Identifiers: To facilitate the distribution of creator rewards and verify identity, we may collect Virtual Payment Addresses (UPI IDs) from eligible creators who meet our platform engagement criteria.

2. Use of Information

Your data helps us maintain a high-quality service:

  • To manage and host digital content for our users.
  • To calculate and distribute Creator Engagement Rewards based on platform performance metrics.
  • To verify the identity of creators to prevent platform abuse and fraud.
  • To process service fee transactions and send related receipts.

3. Reward & Payout Policy

Vayug operates a rewards-based system where credits are allocated for high-engagement content. These credits are managed as platform-specific rewards and can be redeemed once specific verification milestones (such as content upload thresholds) are met. We use the collected UPI information solely for the disbursement of these rewards.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information with third-party payment gateways only as required to process transactions or fulfill reward redemptions. These partners are compliant with PCI-DSS and applicable Indian financial regulations.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your account and payment identifiers. Access to creator payment information is restricted to authorized personnel only for the purpose of processing redemptions.
