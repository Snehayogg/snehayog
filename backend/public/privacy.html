Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Vayug is a digital content hosting and advertising platform that allows users to upload and share user-generated content (UGC). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile information, and device identifiers.
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): Videos, images, captions, and related metadata uploaded to the platform.
  • Advertising Data: Information related to advertising credit purchases and campaign activity.
  • Transaction Information: Payment confirmations and billing records processed through authorized third-party payment gateways. We do not store card or banking details on our servers.
  • Reward Program Information: We may collect additional verification details from eligible participants in the Creator Incentive Program to prevent identity misuse, fraud, duplicate accounts, or manipulation of platform incentives.
  • Moderation & Safety Data: Reports, flags, and related information submitted to maintain community safety.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Vayug platform.
  • To host, distribute, and moderate user-generated content.
  • To process advertising credit purchases.
  • To calculate platform-based engagement rewards under our Creator Incentive Program.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or policy violations.

3. Creator Incentive Program

Vayug operates a discretionary Creator Incentive Program. Incentives are calculated based on platform performance metrics such as views, engagement levels.

Incentives are platform-managed benefits and are not guaranteed earnings, salaries, employment compensation, or financial investment returns.

We may collect additional verification information from eligible participants in the Creator Incentive Program to prevent fraud, identity manipulation, or reward abuse.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with:

  • Authorized payment gateways for transaction processing.
  • Cloud hosting providers for secure data storage.
  • Regulatory authorities when legally required.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption and access control to protect user data. Sensitive financial information is handled only by certified third-party payment processors.

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information by contacting us at the details below.
